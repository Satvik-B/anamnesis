# End-of-Day Review Questions

Ask these one by one. Wait for answer before asking the next. **Skip questions whose required module is disabled.**

## Progress `[daily_rituals]`
1. **Which sprint tasks did you work on today?** *(task numbers from the sprint plan, e.g. #1, #5)*
2. **What activities did you complete or make progress on?** *(specific checkbox items from active-tasks)*
3. **Roughly how much time on each?** *(helps track actuals vs estimates)*

## Blockers & Risks `[daily_rituals]`
4. **Did you hit any blockers or unexpected issues?** *(dependency delays, environment issues, waiting on someone)*
5. **Any risks surfaced that we should track?** *(deadline concerns, scope creep, unknowns)*

## Unplanned Work `[daily_rituals]`
6. **Any ad hoc work that came up?** *(Slack requests, escalations, meetings, code reviews — capture source and rough effort)*

## Collaboration `[daily_rituals]`
7. **Any notable syncs, decisions, or feedback received?** *(meetings, decisions worth recording)*

## Status Changes `[sprint_tracking]`
8. **Any tasks change status?** *(e.g. moved to REVIEW, DONE, or BLOCKED)*

## Personal & Health `[personal]`
9. **Gym today?** *(yes/no — only on gym days)*
10. **Any personal task progress?** *(errands, events, etc. — skip if nothing)*

## Tomorrow `[daily_rituals]`
11. **Anything you want to prioritize or flag for tomorrow?** *(carryover items, meetings, deadlines)*
